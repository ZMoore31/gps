Our Team

Martin Giff

Marty Giff has been active full time with real estate appraisal, brokerage, management and development in Omaha and the Midwest since 1986. Marty is the founder and sole owner of Giff Property Services. He earned his Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration from the University of Nebraska at Lincoln in 1981. He has been a licensed real estate salesman since 1985 and a real estate broker in Nebraska since 2007. He was awarded the Senior Residential Appraiser (SRA) designation from the Appraisal Institute in 1989, and completed extensive commercial courses required for commercial designations. In 1992 when licensing was implemented in Nebraska, Mr. Giff attained the Certified General Real Property Appraiser license. Martin then attained a Certified General Real Property Appraiser license in the the state of Iowa and is now qualified to appraise all property types in the states of Iowa and Nebraska.

Trenton Rengo

Trent Rengo started his career in real estate appraisal and management with Giff Property Services in January 2012. Trent completed his bachelors degree in Businesss Administration with a specialization in Real Estate and Land Use at the University of Nebraska at Omaha in May of 2015. Trent obtained his Certified General Real Property Appraiser License in June of 2015.
